Technology Section

The scholarly journal is the key way that economists communicate with each other.

The way that economist use this scholarly journal is that they find each scholarly journal’s benefits and key information.

The information that they contain in the scholarly journals are different depending on how much they have used

information or their thoughts. Some of the scholarly journal could mainly contain the information of the fact

based materials. However, the scholarly journals that economist write usually involve the information and their assumption.

For example, when Motorola and Google had huge merger, there were significant scholarly journals

about the information of the pricing and the outcome of this merger. In the article of “Tech M&A: Google Buys

Motorola, Time Warner Moves Into the Midwest, HP Buys Autonomy”, it states the specific information of the merger. The

merger price was 12.5 billion dollar worth and CEO of Google stated that the Motorola will run its service by

android. However, Motorola will be running as a separate business (Thompson). As this information appeared to

economists, they gathered multiple scholarly journals about Google’s merger and think of the outcome of this M&A. The

scholarly journals that they write would be a communication method to exchange information within economists.

Another kind of method would be writing the analysis of the economic status of that specific firm. Economists have

to research specific area and write their speculation about the later effect of the economic status. In the

scholarly journal “The Knowledge Economy and Innovation: Certain uncertainty and the risk economy”, it states that

the relationship between the Innovation policy and National systems by connecting with the risk of the economy.

There are multiple risk economy mentioned in the scholarly journal and explain each of the concept specifically (Bullena).

Since the Risk economy affects the investment rate depending on the exchange rates, government

regulation, or political stability, the scholarly journal finally states that the decision should not be made urgently.

This information can be used efficiently by the economist to write their own scholarly journals and since these

scholarly journals are written by many different people, its part of exchanging information and communicating with others.

When reading the scholarly journal or articles, economists have to find key factor of the whole context.

In order to do so, they need to be aware of all the contents to analyze the terms. There are multiple ways of finding information.

Sometimes it is hard for them to read every single detail so they look for key words of each context.

Similar to writing style, reading the referenced statement of other economist’s journal could not only be good

way to hear about other economist’s statement, but also discuss about it and share each others’ information. With

the information that has been gathered, the theory is made as mentioned in the previous paragraphs. For example,

when new technology is invented, there will always be cause and effect. When Samsung releases their new product,

there will be articles and scholarly journals about this product. The articles and scholarly journal could

contain information like “The released product was popular in Korea and the stock went up few percent just in one day”.

The economist would read this information to predict their thoughts on the cause. /p>
