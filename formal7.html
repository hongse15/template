Conclusion

In conclusion, each discipline has their own way of communicating with each other.

It is very hard for the students to find the way to communicate within their discipline when they enter in their area. For

the economist, they have to think broadly. There are multiple factors that affect the economy such as natural

disaster, war, or market crash. Combining all of these factors, Economist have to look at each of these factors and

speculation his or her own theory. That is why economist has to think variety of aspect to obtain information and

come up with a conclusion. The communication method for the economists have is usually similar. Researching

writing and reading information circulates every time. In this particular area, it is very important for economist

to communicate with each other. This means that they have to share information to speculation their own theory

and by referencing other people’s opinion, their connection other economist increases. As a general perspective,

the economist change or affect our quality of lives by giving warning about the future plan or our society’s’

economy. This is good method and useful source for government or any firms to prepare for the future so they can

reduce the risk or avoid the encounter of the worst case scenario.
