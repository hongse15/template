In South Korea, middle school and high school students have certain times that they have to work voluntarily in order to fill their requirement. Some students work in senior citizens' centers to help elders who are physically unhealthy or go to residential facilities to help these disabilities. This not only gave opportunity to help these people, but also students learn how to treat these people in public. Their actions have changed significantly through these voluntarily working hours. Forming all of these skills brought benefits to our society that people changed their negative point of view on not just disabilities, but also people who are different than others.
