Executive Summary

Our society has been diversified with various pop cultural artifacts and some of these artifacts are based on our lives. It affects our lives by talking about these artifacts and for its uniqueness. The movie “Frozen” came out only few weeks ago and it gained its fame in a short period of time. The movie caught people’s eyes because of the music “Let it go”. It was very famous that the song has been translated into 25 different languages. The content of this story is main character Anna has good relationship with her sister Elsa. Elsa has special power to freeze anything and make snow. The story begins with Elsa causing accident where she harms Anna. This accident makes Elsa trap into her room for a long time without ordinary communication with her sister. She had to limit the contact of people since her power was getting stronger by the year. However, Elsa could not avoid contact with others. On the day of Elsa’s coronation, Elsa unfortunately revealed her secret and had to leave the city because of citizen’s point of view. She had no choice but to find a place for her own. This caused problem because Elsa was actually creating snow storm without realizing it. Anna had to go and bring back Elsa to end this snow storm.
