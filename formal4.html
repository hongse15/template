Interview

Economists signify the use of the statistics. The data that economists’ research are always

important to make their decision on writing their scholarly journals. These data’s are the fact based materials

so the result that economists have in mind could mostly be similar to others if they are able to predict the

right speculation (Ballard). However, the speculation will not always be same even if the past data are same. The

answer for the scholarly journal all depends on how well the each of the economist interprets the data. Also,

these economist’s speculations are not always true. The economy cannot be tested like science in order to predict

the answer. The result always varies every time and determines by each situation. Also, when economists’ write

their own assumption, they reference other economist’s journal and agrees or disagrees about their statement (Ballard).

For example, when Mr. Ballard (Charles Ballard - current Professor of economics in Michigan state

university and director of State of the State Survey) assumes that his colleagues’ opinion is not right, he would

include his colleagues name in this journal to state his opinion and include his own thoughts. The Mathematical

terms that economist use is important. In the interview with Charles Ballard, he stated that “There are a lot of

mathematical notations in the journals that we write. With the mathematical terms, we can simulate the economy.

In my master’s degree, I have used this simulation in the computer to find out the simulated economy. The formula

determined by increasing or decreasing many factors such as tax rate, unemployment rate or corporate tax.” The

other sources that economist use to find information is that they use is survey (Ballard). The surveys that

economists use depends the journal that they write. As mentioned before, survey also is a part of data use. In

Michigan, there is multiple survey research and some of the programs would be “State of the State survey” and

“Consumer sentimental index” (Ballard). They would use this information to calculate the mathematical formula for the

simulation of economy. After they have calculated these formulas, the economists state their own theory of their own topic.

After Economists combine their research, they use these statistic, data, graphs, and scholarly journals to write their

own journal. As mentioned before, economist’s job is to interpret these sources from the past to analyze the

economy of future and since they are hard to be testified, most of the speculation has to be simulation. This is called

“Empirical study” (Hong). The main focus of this work is to assume the future based on the past data.

Economy involves every aspects of our society. It relates to every piece of our work and in order to study this

discipline, they need to have plenty of information to analyze their work. Even though they are categorized into

different areas such as political economy, scientific economy or economic liberalism, they are all related to one

purpose. It is to find out the specific details of each area and analyze them as compiled form of data. It mostly

involves the economic situation of that specific nation or the firms that they have researched on and include

their own assumption as a result. The each area also has to discuss the pros and cons of the information. (CareerPlanner.com).
