Introduction

The way that Economist writes information has many different methods. The first thing

that economists do is reading and gathering scholarly journals or articles that other economist wrote. Other than

just scholarly journals and articles, there are other sources that help them to obtain information. There is press

conference made by the US government that announces the economic status about inflation & consumption rate, trade,

unemployment rate and poverty percentage. After stating this information, they talk about what has to be done for the

better future (Hong). It is also a place for the economist to state their speculation and discuss their prospective

future economy. The conference is usually done in quarterly and when economist talks about the economy, they divide

into four sections to separate the terms. Other than governments’ press conference, AEA (American Economic Association)

is good example for economists to gain information (AEA). It is a conference for the economists to exchange

information by making presentation about all the statics and economic status on the world. This organization’s purpose

is to encourage the economic discipline as much as possible for the economist to engage in their discussion and share

their information with others. It is good source for the economist because they not only are able to communicate with

each other, but also they have the opportunity to build their professions by participating in the conference. There

are multiple way that scholarly journal is used. For example, the topic of the scholarly journal about United

Kingdom’s economy could use as a trade journal publication. In this trade publication “The UK Economy: Is the outlook

really that good?”, the content states that economic status of United Kingdom is depended by the US economy (O'Carroll).

The main idea is that even if the United Kingdom’s economy growth is increasing rate, the risk that they face is

unavoidable and even if the United States is able to recover the economy, United Kingdom is unsure of their future

speculation due to their slowly growing rate of unemployment and lower inflation. The other important fact is that the

journal has to include the economists’ own speculation all time. This is the whole point of writing these journals.

The image that each economist’s thought should be included in order to exchange information otherwise the paper that

they wrote would only just an analyzed form of certain topic. So, after economist gathered information about the

United Kingdom’s status,

economists predict the outcome of United Kingdom’s future.
