Research Section

The way that articles and scholarly journal spreads has many different ways.

Most general methods would be spreading through Newspaper, internet or magazine (Hong). In the News paper, there are certain

articles that the economists write. In Korea there is certain sections of the Newspaper that shows the market

status of all the companies and usually in the next page, there are articles that economist write. The information

that has been written in the Newspaper deals with some fact based and speculated information. The magazine is

good example for economist to share their ideas. The Magazine “Economist” is magazine that compiles all the

scholarly journal about multiple topic that economist wrote. The topics are usually about economic situation of firms

or nation’s economy. However, sometimes they include articles about famous person’s story and their interview.

This is also part of sharing their information by talking about his or her opinion on certain topic. The last

important source is by spreading through internet. Internet is very useful source for economist to share their

scholarly journal or articles by uploading in famous web like “NBER”. NBER is not just a researching center, but

they also allow economist to publish their articles (Hong).
